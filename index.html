What is LegacyWoW?

LegacyWoW is a progressive World of Warcraft server that advances naturally through the expansions, preserving each era while introducing meaningful custom systems.

We also feature a unique open-world game mode called Guild War, where guilds go head-to-head controlling key locations throughout Azeroth—creating persistent conflict, rivalries, and player-driven objectives.
