Guild War Expansion & Server Stability Updates
Guild War continues to evolve across Azeroth.

Six additional outposts have now been added to the Guild War system. These locations are fully capturable and expand the battlefield for guilds competing for territory.

  • 6 new capturable outpost locations
  • Expanded Guild War control across Azeroth

In addition to the new outposts, we have implemented major backend improvements to improve the experience for everyone.

  • Large improvements to overall server stability
  • Major updates to world phasing systems
  • Optimizations for large-scale guild conflicts

More Guild War locations and features are planned as the world continues to evolve.
