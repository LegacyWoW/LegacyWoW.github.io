Stress Test Complete — Closed Alpha Begins January 22
Posted January 22, 2026 · Announcement Alpha

The LegacyWoW stress test has officially concluded. Thank you to everyone who participated and helped us push the server to its limits.

We are excited to announce that Closed Alpha will begin on January 22, 2026.

The Closed Alpha will be limited to 500 players and will focus on core gameplay stability, progression pacing, and early custom content.

Additional information on access and expectations will be shared soon. We appreciate everyone’s patience and support as we move into the next phase of LegacyWoW.
