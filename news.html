March 22, 2026

Alpha Phase 1 Complete

We are excited to announce that Phase 1 of our Alpha has officially been completed!

We want to extend a huge thank you to everyone who participated and provided valuable feedback throughout this phase. Your input is incredibly important, and we will be implementing changes as we move forward into Phase 2 at a later date.

For now, the server will remain offline until Phase 2 is officially announced.

When Phase 2 begins, all 750 participants from Phase 1 will be automatically reinvited and will pick up right where they left off.

In addition, we will be opening the doors to 250 new participants to join the next phase.

Stay tuned for more updates!
