GuildWar Support Pause: July 24–31

We'd like to inform everyone that GuildWar support will be temporarily paused from July 24 through July 31 while members of the development team are away on a scheduled vacation.

During this time, both the Icecrown and Darrowmere realms will remain online and fully playable. Players are welcome to continue leveling, raiding, PvPing, completing quests, and enjoying all other aspects of the game as normal.

However, no official GuildWar progression will take place during this period. This includes territory progression, administrative support, event oversight, and any GuildWar-related rulings or updates.

GuildWar support will officially resume on August 1, 2026, when our team returns. At that time, progression will continue exactly where it left off.

We appreciate everyone's understanding and patience. Taking this short break allows our team to recharge so we can continue delivering the best possible experience for the LegacyWoW community.

Thank you for your continued support, and we'll see you back on the battlefield when GuildWar resumes on August 1st!

May 11, 2026

Challenge Modes are live!

We’re excited to announce an addition to our realms: Challenge Modes!

Challenge Modes are designed for players seeking a more difficult and rewarding journey through Azeroth. Whether you want to test your survival skills, limit your progression, or take on the ultimate Iron Man experience, there’s a challenge waiting for you.

Available Challenges

Hardcore

Players who die become permanently dead and remain ghosts forever. Resurrection is not possible.

Semi-Hardcore

Upon death, players lose all equipped gear and all carried gold.

Self Crafted

Players may only equip items that they have personally crafted.

Item Quality Level

Players may only wear Poor or Normal quality equipment.

Slow XP Gain

Players receive only 0.5x normal experience gains.

Very Slow XP Gain

Players receive only 0.25x normal experience gains.

Quest XP Only

Experience can only be earned through completing quests.

Iron Man Mode

Enforces the full Iron Man ruleset for the ultimate challenge experience.

Activating Challenge Modes Challenges can be activated on a per-character basis by interacting with the Shrine of Challenge located near the graveyard in each starting zone. Please note: Challenges may only be enabled on characters at Level 1. Multiple challenges can be combined, provided they do not conflict with one another. Some combinations, such as Hardcore and Semi-Hardcore, are not allowed together.

Rewards Players who complete Challenge Modes will earn exclusive rewards, including: Unique Items Special Titles Bonus Talent Points Prestige Mode

Important Restrictions Once a challenge has been activated, it cannot be removed until the challenge has been completed. Challenge Mode characters are also separated from the standard playerbase. This means: No trading No grouping No inspecting No standard player interaction You may only interact with other players currently participating in Challenge Modes.

Prepare yourself, adventurer. The road ahead will not be easy.

-LegacyWoW Team
