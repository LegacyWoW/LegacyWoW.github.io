Rules

Keep it fair, keep it fun. These rules are here to protect the community and the integrity of progression.

1) Respect

  • No harassment, hate speech, or targeted abuse.
  • No doxxing, threats, or encouraging self-harm.
  • Keep chat readable: excessive spam is not allowed.

2) Cheating & Exploits

  • No bots, automation, rotation scripts, or gameplay macros that play for you.
  • No third-party hacks (speed, fly, teleport, etc.).
  • No exploiting bugs for advantage (report them instead).

3) Economy & Trading

  • No real-money trading (RMT) of gold, items, or accounts.
  • No scamming other players.

4) Account Responsibility

  • You are responsible for your account’s actions.
  • Don’t share your password. Use a unique password.

5) Naming

  • No offensive or hateful names.
  • Staff may rename characters that violate rules.
Note: LegacyWoW is progressive from patch 1.0.0 to 3.3.5a with custom content along the way. Any additional rules for specific seasons, events, or custom systems will be posted in News.
