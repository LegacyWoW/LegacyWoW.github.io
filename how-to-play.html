How to Play

LegacyWoW runs on the 3.3.5a client and progresses from patch 1.0.0 → 3.3.5a, with custom content along the way. Follow the steps below and you’ll be in-game fast.

1) Get the 3.3.5a client

Install or extract a clean World of Warcraft 3.3.5a client. (No extra patches required unless we announce otherwise.)

2) Set your realmlist

Find your realmlist.wtf file. Common locations for 3.3.5a include:

World of Warcraft\Data\enUS\realmlist.wtf World of Warcraft\Data\enGB\realmlist.wtf

Open it with Notepad and set it to:

set realmlist 25.39.161.60

Tip: if you have multiple language folders (enUS/enGB/etc), make sure you edit the one your client uses.

3) Create your account

Create your account on our website:

Create Account

4) Log in and play

Run Wow.exe, log in with your new account, and you’re set.

If you get stuck, check the FAQ first — it covers the most common login and realmlist issues.
