How to Play

LegacyWoW runs on the 3.3.5a client and progresses from patch 1.0.0 → 3.3.5a.

Home Create Account News Rules FAQ

1) Get the Client

Use a clean World of Warcraft 3.3.5a client.

2) Set your realmlist

set realmlist 25.39.161.60

3) Create an Account

Use the Create Account page to generate the AzerothCore command. An admin pastes it into the worldserver console.

4) Play

Launch Wow.exe and log in.
